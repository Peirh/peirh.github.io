Aren't cognates cool?
------

An interesting Finnish-Manchu cognate: the Finnish word hiiri "mouse", from Proto-Uralic *šiŋere, is a cognate to the Manchu word singgeri "mouse", from Proto-Tungusic: *siŋere.
